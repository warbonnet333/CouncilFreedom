Save Ukraine all together: humanitarian & medical help for injured citizens during the War

Catastrophe description in Ukraine

24thof February 2022 year Russian Federation had started military attack to the territory of Ukraine. Armed forces of Ukraine has been fighting off attacks of Russia every day, but air bombing attacks against peaceful cities are still going on, destroying residential quarters in cities and ignoring to provide humanitarian corridors for civil people.

2 000 civil citizens killed during several fist weeks of the War, preliminary the same quantity are injured, 2 000 000 Ukrainian citizens were forced to leave Ukraine to survive.

The whole world is united to stop bloody dictator. Discover what you can do to help with that.

Fundraising for humanitarian help

The War has stopped to organize supplying the most important – medicines and food. Many people has lost their homes, hiding in refuges, but resources are limited. We are doing all our best to prevent humanitarian crisis. Join to rescue people.

Humanitarian aid collection

Any help is useful. Food, clothes, medicines, medicine equipment, first aid kits - any that can help injured people and refugees on the territory of War attacks. You can collect humanitarian help to your own solution and send us- we will sort it and pass to whom needs in it more.

Humanitarian aid receiving

You lost your home, you are forced to leave home or hiding to survive? We can help you to receive humanitarian aid.

Money collection for ruined buildings’ reconstruction and infrastructure

According to KSE’s and volunteers’ information approximately 200 educational institutions, 30 medical institutions, 8 churches, 1600 residential buildings, 19 administrative buildings, 23 plants, 12 airfields, 5 heat power plants and 5 hydro power plants were partially / fully destroyed.

Besides 15 000 km highways, 5 000 km roads, 350 bridges were partially or fully destroyed

About the organization

The Council of Freedom is an association of free-spirited people with an active position in life.

The international NGO “Council of Freedom” was established to implement charitable, social and humanitarian projects.

Our main ideology has always been to make people a little happier and the world kinder and better. At such a difficult time for our country, we cannot stay away, and we focus all our efforts and other caring people on helping and supporting those in need, becoming hostages of the brutal injustice that has changed the lives of every Ukrainian and people around the world. the world forever.
